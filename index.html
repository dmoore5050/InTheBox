What's in the box?

User Registration

Reward users for signing up/connecting with a campaign. Users can register by connecting with Facebook or by manually entering name/email fields. (Facebook is the only social network that can currently create users via registration).

Details

  • Custom form
  • Can include age minimum, phone, zip
  • Can include age gate
  • Facebook connect encouraged

Account Sync

Reward users for connecting social accounts. Users authenticate the client for various permissions, allowing for tracking and rewarding activity.

Supported networks

  • Facebook (can act as registration)
  • Twitter
  • Instagram
  • LinkedIn
  • Foursquare

Like/Follow

Reward users for become a fan/follow of social accounts. Following on Facebook/Twitter is most accurately tracked and rewarded, following accounts on social networks with closed APIs can be encouraged and rewarded upon clickthrough, or through embeddable follow buttons.

Details

  • Can reward for Likes/Follows retroactively
  • Full integration with Facebook/Twitter

Social Posts

Reward users for posting a specific message on Facebook and Twitter. Users are encouraged to post a given word/phrase on Facebook or Twitter, rewarded when we track them posting the “trigger.”

Details

  • Typically included in auto-prompt
  • Users must be connected to track

Hashtags

Reward users for posting specific Hashtags on Facebook and Twitter. Users are encouraged to post a given Hashtag on Facebook or Twitter, rewarded when we track them posting the “trigger.”

Details

  • Typically included in auto-prompt
  • Users must be connected to track

Photo Posts

Reward users for posting a photo to Facebook, Twitter, and/or Instagram with a specific phrase or hashtag. Same rules apply as Social posts, and the posts are identified as including an image. Photos can be pulled into a gallery.

Details

  • Curated gallery
  • Photos can be "featured" (larger)

Instagram Video Submission

Reward users for posting a video to Instagram with a specific phrase or hashtag. Same rules apply as Social posts, and the posts are identified as including a video. Photos can be pulled into a gallery.

Details

  • Curated gallery
  • Submissions can be "votable"

View/Listen to Content

Reward users for watching a video, listening to an audio file, or consuming another piece of timed content. After a predetermined amount of time, the user is rewarded.

Example content

  • YouTube
  • Soundcloud
  • Vimeo

Refer Friends

Reward users for referring their friends to join a campaign. Users are given a specific link to share with friends that directs to the campaign. Upon the friend signing up, the original user is rewarded.

Details

  • Rewarded upon signup, not just link clicks (to reduce spam)
  • Links can be spread through email, social networks, etc.

Location-Based Checkins

Reward users for checking in at one or more predetermined locations. Assign as many locations as desired into a set group, and reward users based on checking in at locations that qualify in the group.

Supported networks

  • Facebook
  • Foursquare

Quizzes and Questionnaires

Reward users for answering a poll/quiz. Poll results may be tracked and used to classify users. Quizzes can present the users with specific pieces of content based on their responses.

Example use cases

  • Voting/Ranking via poll
  • Assigning users to groups based on poll response
  • Self-fulfilling quizzes (“What type of ____ are you?”)
  • Trivia quizzes

Code/Text Entry

Reward users for entering text as part of a promotion. Users can enter “free text” to provide personalized submissions, or they can be required to enter specific text (secret codes, correct answers, etc.).

Details

  • Redeem codes
  • Misc. survey response
  • Enter “passcode” to view content

Miscellaneous

Reward users for participating/engaging with various other forms of content, including UGC, clickthroughs, and some interactive games.

Example use cases

  • Click through to POS
  • Slot-machine-style game
  • Upload+caption images
